Innovation In Isolation

The year 2020 witnessed a prolonged lockdown and in this due course of time from assignments to classes everything went online. E-Cell too used the online platform to conduct an event "Innovation in Isolation" in this period to engage students in innovation and make this time quite fun for them. The event was organised in four categories being "Cooking", "Art and Craft", "Photography" and "Project Presentation". January 2020

Aarambh Series

It is a series of interviews with the successful entrepreneurs of chhattisgarh exploring their entrepreneurial journey and their struggles. Our first story is about "NUKKAD : THE TEA CAFE", a place that is known for it's unique style of service and hospitality. The founder and chairperson Mr Priyank Patel had a vision to give an identity to specially abled people of our society which he achieved by years of struggle, hardwork and determination. The objective of this series is to motivate fellow aspiring entrepreneurs so that their will to create an impact in the society through their innovation becomes stronger.
